FERNWOOD

Fernwood Farm is a historic 6 acre property outside of Cazenovia, New York. Built in 1874 by a member of the Linklaen family to raise Guernsey Cattle, its main house sat in disrepair until renovation efforts prepared it for its new life as a Bed & Breakfast. We're excited to share this day with you and with Fernwood Farm as their very first wedding!
